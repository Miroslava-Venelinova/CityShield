# **CityShield**

## What is CityShield?
CityShield is a mobile application that provides up-to-date information about power, water, and other utility outages in urban areas in Varna, Bulgaria.
## Main features & Strengths
- Users receive real-time notifications about any interruptions in essential infrastructure, based on official sources such as energy and water supply companies, local authorities, and other trusted institutions. 
**_users will be informed in a timely manner about possible inconveniences, allowing them to plan their daily lives more effectively and prepare for situations such as accidents or repairs_**

- CityShield guarantees the reliability and accuracy of the information by providing up-to-date data directly from official channels to ensure maximum transparency and comfort for users.
- In addition, the chatbot in the app is available 24/7 to answer questions and provide information on the current status of services.
- **_CityShield includes an interactive map that visually marks all current issues._**
